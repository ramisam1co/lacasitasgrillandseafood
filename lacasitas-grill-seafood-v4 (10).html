Our Story

The Best Mexican Food & Seafood
in El Cajon, CA

La Casita's Grill & Seafood is a multi-generational family-owned restaurant in El Cajon beloved by the entire East County San Diego community. From our famous carne asada fries and surf & turf to fresh shrimp dishes and crispy rolled tacos, every bite is made with love, fresh ingredients, and generations of Mexican culinary tradition.

Carne Asada Done Right

Tender, perfectly seasoned carne asada in burritos, tacos, fries, and plates. El Cajon's most-searched dish.

Fresh Mexican Seafood

Camarones al mojo de ajo, ceviche tostada, shrimp burritos, caldo de camaron — seafood lovers rejoice.

Breakfast Served All Day

Our legendary breakfast burritos with beans, eggs & your choice of meat — served any time, every day.

Homemade Churros

Fresh churros with fresa crema, cajeta, caramel, or Bavarian cream — just $1.99. A true El Cajon treat.
